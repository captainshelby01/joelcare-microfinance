FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

We offer loans to business owners (traders, SME retail operators) and salary earners (civil servants or employees of reputable private organizations) residing in Sapele and surrounding Delta State cities.
Once you provide all the basic details over WhatsApp, we aim to issue a credit decision in under 24 hours. For return borrowers, decisions are often faster, sometimes within a few hours.
We operate on a transparent flat interest rate model (e.g. 5% flat per month depending on the category and term). Final rate allocations are calculated and confirmed after a brief WhatsApp consultation.
For salary earners, we do not require physical collateral (we verify bank statements, salary history, and identity records). For business owners, we perform a routine verification of the business shop or inventory.
Repayments can be structured as weekly or monthly installments. Payments are typically made via direct bank transfer to our corporate account. Details are provided in the loan agreement.
